The Village Apartments Building 2 is located at 609 Apartment Lane SW in Forest Lake, Minnesota, 55025. It is a multi-family residence constructed in 1971. The property spans approximately 24,714 square feet and occupies a 1.23-acre lot.

Designated as a multi-family dwelling with five or more units, the building is part of the Gaughan Apartments subdivision. The lot dimensions are approximately 225 feet in depth and 238 feet in frontage.

The property falls within the Forest Lake Public School District. Nearby schools include Forest View Elementary School (grades PreK-3) located about 0.4 miles away, and Forest Lake Senior High School (grades 9-12) approximately 1 mile from the property.
