The Village Apartments Forest Lake, MN Reviews!













































Reviews for Village Apartments in Forest Lake, Minnesota, are generally negative. 26 out of 51 Google reviews are 1 star, more than half the reviews are the lowest possible to leave a review.

Many residents have expressed frustration with the property's overall condition, citing its outdated appearance and lack of meaningful updates. The buildings, originally constructed in the 1970s, have received minimal improvements over the years, aside from new carpeting in some areas. Several tenants have noted that the hallways and entrances often appear dirty, which contributes to an unwelcoming atmosphere.

Maintenance issues are a recurring complaint, with residents reporting long wait times for necessary repairs. Some have stated that management is unresponsive or slow to address concerns, leaving tenants to deal with broken appliances, plumbing issues, and other problems for extended periods. The lack of consistent upkeep has led to dissatisfaction among those who expect prompt and efficient service from property management.

Safety is another concern for tenants at Village Apartments. Some residents have reported incidents of car break-ins and other property crimes, raising worries about security in the complex. The local area is known for its crime rate, with property crime being more common than violent crime. Additionally, there are complaints about unpleasant odors in the buildings, which some attribute to the high number of pet owners and inadequate cleaning efforts.

Overall, the majority of negative reviews highlight issues related to maintenance, management, cleanliness, and security. These concerns have led to a general sense of dissatisfaction among residents, making Village Apartments a less appealing option for those seeking quality housing in Forest Lake, Minnesota.

While many reviews for Village Apartments in Forest Lake, Minnesota, are negative, some residents have shared positive experiences.

A few tenants appreciate the affordability of the complex, noting that the rent is lower compared to other housing options in the area. While some residents once appreciated Village Apartments for its affordability, many now note that rent has increased significantly and is nearly the same as newer, more updated buildings in the area.

Some residents have also mentioned that the location is a strong selling point. The complex is conveniently situated near local shopping centers, restaurants, and parks, making it easy for tenants to access daily necessities and recreational activities. A handful of reviewers have also complimented the pet-friendly policy, which allows tenants to have animals without excessive restrictions. For those who have had positive experiences, these aspects help balance out some of the property's shortcomings.
